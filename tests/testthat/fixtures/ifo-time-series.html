ifo Time-series

With its business surveys the ifo Institute collects data that are of great interest for empirical economic research both in Germany and abroad. Some long time-series are provided in Excel format for facilitating further processing by the user.

ifo Business Survey

  • ifo Business Climate (April 2024)
  • ifo Export Expectations (March 2024)
  • ifo Employment Barometer for Germany (March 2024)
  • ifo Business Climate for Eastern Germany (March 2024)
  • ifo Business Climate Saxony (March 2024)

Historical Time Series

  • ifo Business Survey: Historical Time Series for Western Germany (1960-1990)
  • ifo World Economic Climate (1990-2019)
  • ifo Economic Climate for the Euro Area (1990-2019)

Content

ifo Business Climate

Germany:
Index (2015=100, seasonally adjusted): Business Climate, Business Situation, Business Expectations.
Balances (seasonally adjusted): Business Climate, Business Situation, Business Expectations.
ifo Dispersion (seasonally adjusted and three months moving average).

Manufacturing, Services, Trade, Construction:
Balances (seasonally adjusted): Business Climate, Business Situation, Business Expectations.

ifo Business Climate for Eastern Germany

Index (2015=100, seasonally adjusted): Business Climate, Business Situation, Business Expectations.

ifo World Economic Climate
ifo Economic Climate for the Euro Area

Balances: Economic Climate Euro Area, Present Situation, Expectations next 6 months

ifo Export Climate

Ifo Export Climate (index values, seasonally-adjusted); Special trade exports (annual rate of change, real, seasonally-adjusted)
